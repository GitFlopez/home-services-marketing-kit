# Marketing Plan: Home Services & Contractor Marketing Kit

## ClawHub Marketplace Listing

**Title:** Home Services & Contractor Marketing Kit — Seasonal Campaigns, Service Pages, Reviews & Social

**Short Description (150 chars):**
Full marketing kit for HVAC, plumbing, electrical, roofing & landscaping: campaign copy, SEO service pages, review system, 30-day social calendar.

**Full Description:**

Home service businesses spend $1,500–$5,000/month on local marketing agencies for the same deliverables this skill produces in 20 minutes for $29.

4 prompts cover everything:

**Prompt 1 — Seasonal Campaign:** Email sequence (3 emails × 3 subject lines), Google Search ad (RSA format), Facebook/Instagram ad, Nextdoor post, and door hanger copy — all built around one seasonal offer (summer AC tune-up, fall furnace check, spring landscaping, pool opening/closing).

**Prompt 2 — Service Page Copy:** Complete SEO-optimized web page with page title, meta description, H1, above-the-fold copy, benefits section, service details (included/not included), How It Works, 5-question FAQ, trust signals, and dual-CTA section.

**Prompt 3 — Review & Referral System:** 3-touch review request sequence (SMS + email + final SMS), QR code card copy, referral program structure with reward logic, and 6 Google review response templates (5-star × 2, 3-star × 2, 1-star × 2).

**Prompt 4 — 30-Day Social Calendar:** 22 posts across Facebook, Instagram, and Nextdoor with full captions, hashtags, visual direction, 2 Reels scripts, and 5 reusable evergreen templates.

**Who this is for:**
✓ HVAC companies, plumbers, electricians, roofers, landscapers, pool/pest/cleaning services
✓ Single-location owner-operators AND multi-location franchise owners
✓ Marketing agencies managing home service clients (white-label friendly)
✓ Contractors launching a new location or slow-season push

**What you DON'T need:** A marketing agency, copywriter, or $49/month AI subscription. Just your business name, city, and current offer.

## Target Buyer Personas

**Persona A: Owner-Operator HVAC / Plumbing (Primary)**
- 5–25 employees, $500K–$3M revenue
- Knows they need marketing but hates writing copy
- Pain: "I spent $2K/month on an agency and don't know what I'm paying for"
- Trigger: Summer cooling season starting, urgent need to book tune-ups NOW

**Persona B: Marketing Agency / Freelancer**
- Manages 3–10 home service clients
- Needs fast, consistent deliverables that sound local
- Will run all 4 prompts per client, charge $500–2,000 per deliverable set

**Persona C: Home Service Franchise Owner**
- Runs 2–5 locations (same brand, different cities)
- Needs customized local content for each market without starting from scratch
- Will pay for agency tier ($197/3 businesses)
