# Skill #97: Home Services & Contractor Marketing Kit Generator

**Category:** Local Business Marketing  
**Difficulty:** Beginner  
**Time to run:** 15–20 minutes per business  
**Output:** Full marketing kit — seasonal campaign, service page copy, referral/review system, 30-day social content calendar

---

## What This Skill Does

Generates a complete marketing kit for home service businesses: HVAC, plumbing, electrical, roofing, landscaping, pool service, pest control, cleaning, and handyman services.

4 prompts cover the entire local marketing lifecycle:
- **Prompt 1:** Seasonal campaign (urgency-driven promotions tied to weather/seasonality)
- **Prompt 2:** Service page copy (SEO-optimized web copy for each service offered)
- **Prompt 3:** Referral & review system (review request sequences + referral program)
- **Prompt 4:** 30-day social media content calendar (platform-specific posts for Facebook, Instagram, Nextdoor)

---

## Inputs Required

- Business name and city/neighborhood
- Service type(s) (HVAC, plumbing, electrical, roofing, landscaping, pool, pest, cleaning, handyman)
- Current season and primary service focus
- Any current promotions, certifications, or differentiators
- Years in business and typical service area (zip codes or neighborhoods)

## Prompt 1: Seasonal Campaign Generator

**Best for:** HVAC summer tune-ups, fall furnace checks, spring landscaping, holiday lighting, pool openings/closings

**Output:**
- Campaign headline + 3 hook variants
- Offer structure (discount, bonus, free inspection)
- Email campaign (3 emails: announce → reminder → last chance)
- Paid ad copy (Google Search headline/description + Facebook primary/headline/CTA)
- Nextdoor post
- Door hanger / postcard copy (front + back)

**Prompt:**

```
You are a local marketing expert specializing in home service businesses. Generate a complete seasonal marketing campaign for the following business.

BUSINESS INFO:
- Name: [Business Name]
- Location: [City, State]
- Service Type: [HVAC / Plumbing / Electrical / Roofing / Landscaping / Pool / Pest / Cleaning / Handyman]
- Campaign Focus: [e.g., "AC tune-up before summer", "fall furnace check", "spring lawn refresh", "pool opening"]
- Current Offer: [e.g., "$89 AC tune-up", "free estimate", "10% off first service", "free filter replacement with tune-up"]
- Differentiators: [e.g., "NATE-certified techs", "same-day service", "100% satisfaction guarantee", "locally owned 15 years"]
- Target Neighborhoods: [e.g., "Summerlin, Henderson, North Las Vegas"]

Generate:

1. CAMPAIGN HEADLINE + HOOKS (3 variants)
   - Primary: urgency + benefit
   - Variant B: fear of missing peak season
   - Variant C: social proof + savings angle
   Rule: Every headline must include a specific number, timeframe, or dollar amount.

2. OFFER STRUCTURE
   - Core offer with pricing
   - Bonus/add-on (free visual inspection, free filter, 15-point checklist)
   - Urgency mechanism (deadline, limited slots, price increase date)
   - Guarantee language

3. EMAIL CAMPAIGN (3-email sequence)
   Email 1 — Announce (Day 0): Subject line + preview text + body (150-200 words)
   Email 2 — Reminder (Day 5): Subject line + preview text + body (100-150 words, reference open window)
   Email 3 — Last Chance (Day 10): Subject line + preview text + body (75-100 words, explicit deadline)
   Rule: Each email must have 3 subject line options.

4. PAID AD COPY
   Google Search Ad:
   - Headline 1 (30 chars): service + location
   - Headline 2 (30 chars): offer/price
   - Headline 3 (30 chars): trust signal
   - Description 1 (90 chars): benefit + CTA
   - Description 2 (90 chars): urgency + guarantee

   Facebook/Instagram Ad:
   - Primary text (125 chars): hook + offer
   - Headline (40 chars): core benefit
   - Description (30 chars): urgency
   - CTA button: [Book Now / Get Quote / Call Now]

5. NEXTDOOR POST (200 words max)
   - Neighbor-first tone (not ad copy)
   - Local reference (neighborhood name, community landmark)
   - Offer embedded naturally
   - Response prompt at end

6. DIRECT MAIL COPY (door hanger or postcard)
   Front: Headline + offer + phone number
   Back: 3 bullet benefits + guarantee + QR code placeholder + "Serving [Neighborhood] since [Year]"
   Rule: Under 75 words total (legibility for quick reads)
```

## Prompt 2: Service Page Copy Generator

**Best for:** HVAC installation, plumbing repair, electrical panel upgrades, roof replacement, landscaping design, pool remodel

**Output:**
- Page title (SEO-optimized, 55-60 chars)
- Meta description (150-160 chars)
- H1 headline
- Above-the-fold copy (60-80 words)
- 3-column benefits section (icons + copy)
- Service details section (what's included, what's NOT included)
- How It Works (3-4 steps)
- FAQ section (5 Q&As)
- Trust section (certifications, guarantees, insurance)
- CTA section (primary + secondary)

**Prompt:**

```
You are a conversion copywriter specializing in local home service websites. Write a complete SEO-optimized service page for the following business and service.

BUSINESS INFO:
- Company Name: [Name]
- City/Metro: [City, State]
- Service: [Specific service, e.g., "AC Replacement", "Drain Cleaning", "Electrical Panel Upgrade", "Roof Repair"]
- Primary Keyword: [e.g., "AC replacement Las Vegas", "emergency plumber Henderson NV"]
- Secondary Keywords: [2-3 related terms]
- Pricing (if public): [e.g., "$89 flat fee", "free estimates", "$150-300 typical range"]
- Certifications: [e.g., "NATE-certified", "licensed C-10", "GAF Master Elite", "licensed and bonded"]
- Guarantees: [e.g., "1-year labor warranty", "100% satisfaction or free return visit"]
- Years in Business: [X years]

Generate:

1. SEO METADATA
   - Page Title (55-60 chars): [Primary keyword] | [Company Name] | [City]
   - Meta Description (150-160 chars): benefit + offer + local trust signal + CTA

2. PAGE HEADLINE (H1)
   - Must include city name and core benefit
   - Format: [Action] + [Service] in [City] — [Benefit/Proof]
   - Example: "Fast AC Replacement in Las Vegas — Same-Day Installs, 10-Year Warranty"

3. ABOVE-THE-FOLD COPY (60-80 words)
   - Lead with the customer's primary pain (not the company)
   - Include: who this is for, what they get, why trust you
   - End with phone number prompt

4. BENEFITS SECTION (3 columns)
   Icon label + 1-sentence benefit statement (each under 20 words)
   Focus: speed, reliability, price transparency, warranty

5. SERVICE DETAILS
   What's Included (bulleted list, 6-8 items)
   What's NOT Included (2-3 items — builds trust through honesty)
   Typical Job Duration: [X hours / half-day / full day]

6. HOW IT WORKS (3-4 numbered steps)
   Each step: title (4-5 words) + 1-sentence description
   Total reading time under 60 seconds

7. FAQ SECTION (5 Q&As)
   Q1: "How much does [service] cost in [city]?"
   Q2: "How long does [service] take?"
   Q3: "Do I need to be home?"
   Q4: "Is your work guaranteed?"
   Q5: [Most common objection for this specific service]
   Rule: Each answer 40-75 words — specific, no corporate speak

8. TRUST SIGNALS SECTION
   - Certifications list (formatted as badges)
   - Insurance statement: "Licensed, bonded, and insured in [State]"
   - BBB / Google rating placeholder (if applicable)
   - Guarantee box: exact language for service guarantee

9. CTA SECTION
   Primary CTA: call/text/book online (phone number prominent)
   Secondary CTA: "Get a Free Estimate" form teaser (3 fields: name, address, service needed)
   Urgency line: [e.g., "Most installs completed same-week. Book before [month] slots fill."]
```

## Prompt 3: Referral & Review System

**Best for:** Building Google review count, generating referral leads from happy customers

**Output:**
- Post-service review request sequence (3-touch: text → email → final ask)
- QR code card copy (leave-behind)
- Referral program structure + reward
- Referral card copy (physical + digital)
- Response templates for 1-star, 3-star, and 5-star Google reviews (6 templates)

**Prompt:**

```
You are a customer experience strategist for local home service businesses. Build a complete referral and review system for the following business.

BUSINESS INFO:
- Company Name: [Name]
- Service Type: [HVAC / Plumbing / etc.]
- City: [City, State]
- Current Google Reviews: [e.g., 47 reviews, 4.2 stars]
- Referral Reward (if any): [e.g., "$25 Visa gift card", "$50 off next service", "free filter replacement"]
- Primary Contact Method: [text, email, phone call]
- Timing: [review requests sent same day, next day, or 48 hours after service]

Generate:

1. POST-SERVICE REVIEW REQUEST SEQUENCE (3-touch)

   Touch 1 — SMS (30 min after tech leaves job site):
   [Under 160 chars. No exclamation points. Conversational tone. Includes technician's first name.]
   
   Touch 2 — Email (24 hours later, only if no review yet):
   Subject line + preview text + body (80-100 words)
   Rule: Reference the specific service performed (variable: [SERVICE_PERFORMED])
   
   Touch 3 — Final SMS Ask (72 hours later, only if no review yet):
   [Under 130 chars. Last ask — no further follow-up after this.]

2. REVIEW QR CARD COPY (leave-behind card, business card size)
   Front: "How'd We Do?" + rating graphic placeholder + QR code placeholder
   Back: 3-bullet "What to include in your review" (guides customers to write better reviews)
   Rule: Under 60 words total

3. REFERRAL PROGRAM STRUCTURE
   Program name (2-3 words, memorable)
   Reward for referrer: [cash / discount / gift card — specific amount]
   Reward for new customer: [discount on first service]
   Program rules (3 bullets — simple enough to explain in 30 seconds)
   Expiration policy

4. REFERRAL CARD COPY (physical leave-behind + digital version)
   Physical card (business card size):
   - Front: Program name + "Give your neighbor [X]" + company contact
   - Back: How to redeem + expiration + referral code placeholder [REF-XXXXX]
   
   Digital version (text message copy):
   [Under 160 chars. Easy to forward. Includes booking link placeholder.]

5. GOOGLE REVIEW RESPONSE TEMPLATES (6 templates)

   5-star glowing review (2 variants):
   Template A: Acknowledge specifics + community angle
   Template B: Reinforce service + subtle CTA to share
   
   3-star "it was fine" review (2 variants):
   Template A: Thank + invite offline conversation
   Template B: Acknowledge + offer to make right without defensiveness
   
   1-star negative review (2 variants):
   Template A: De-escalate + move offline + no admissions
   Template B: Factual + empathetic + next-step focused
   Rule: No review response exceeds 120 words. No "we take pride in" or "we're sorry you feel that way."
```

## Prompt 4: 30-Day Social Media Content Calendar

**Best for:** Facebook, Instagram, and Nextdoor — the top 3 platforms for home service local marketing

**Output:**
- 30-day calendar (4-5 posts/week, 18-22 total posts)
- Each post: platform, format, caption, hashtags
- 3 evergreen content pillars for year-round use
- 5 complete caption templates (reusable)

**Prompt:**

```
You are a social media strategist for local home service businesses. Build a 30-day content calendar and reusable post system for the following business.

BUSINESS INFO:
- Company Name: [Name]
- Service Type: [HVAC / Plumbing / Electrical / Roofing / Landscaping / Pool / Pest / Cleaning]
- Location: [City, State — note any major neighborhoods, landmarks, seasons]
- Target Customer: [Homeowners / Renters / Property Managers / All three]
- Brand Tone: [Friendly-expert / No-nonsense-professional / Community-neighbor / Premium-luxury]
- Current Season: [Spring / Summer / Fall / Winter] — include current local weather context
- Active Platforms: [Facebook / Instagram / Nextdoor / All three]
- Content Restrictions: [e.g., "no job site before/after (no permission from homeowners)", "no employee faces", "no pricing in posts"]

Generate:

1. CONTENT PILLARS (3 pillars for year-round use)
   Each pillar: name + description + 5 post ideas
   
   Pillar A: Education (builds authority)
   Pillar B: Social Proof (reviews, before/afters, stats)
   Pillar C: Community + Seasonal (local relevance, seasonality)
   Rule: Every pillar must produce at least 2 posts that work on Nextdoor specifically.

2. 30-DAY CALENDAR (Weeks 1–4)
   Format for each post entry:
   - Day/Date
   - Platform: [Facebook / Instagram / Nextdoor]
   - Format: [Photo / Reel / Carousel / Text post]
   - Hook (first 125 chars — critical for Facebook feed stop-scroll)
   - Full Caption (Facebook: 150-200 words / Instagram: 100-150 words + hashtags / Nextdoor: 100-150 words neighbor-tone)
   - Hashtags (Instagram only: 10-15, mix of local + service + generic)
   - Visuals direction: what photo or graphic to use (1 sentence)
   
   Calendar distribution:
   - Week 1: Introduce seasonal campaign, 1 educational post, 1 social proof post
   - Week 2: 2 educational, 1 community/local, 1 offer reminder
   - Week 3: FAQ series (3 posts answering common questions), 1 before/after
   - Week 4: Final campaign push, 1 team/behind-scenes, 1 ask for referral
   
   Rule: At least 4 posts must be Nextdoor-native (neighbor tone, local references, no overt promotion).
   Rule: Include 2 Reels concepts with script outline (15-30 sec, voiceover + b-roll directions).

3. 5 EVERGREEN TEMPLATE CAPTIONS (reusable any time of year)
   Template 1: "[Season] Tip" — single home maintenance tip, educational
   Template 2: "Did You Know?" — surprising stat or fact about [service type]
   Template 3: "Review Spotlight" — formatted customer review highlight
   Template 4: "Team Spotlight" — introduce a technician (placeholder: [TECH_NAME], [YEARS_EXPERIENCE], [FUN_FACT])
   Template 5: "This Happened Last Week" — job story format (no identifying info) → lesson for homeowner
   Rule: Each template is self-contained, under 175 words, ready to post with only the placeholder filled in.

4. QUICK-POST FORMULA CHEAT SHEET (1 page)
   5 post types that take under 10 minutes to create:
   - Photo + 3-sentence caption formula
   - "Tip Tuesday" text post formula
   - Review screenshot post formula
   - Seasonal warning post formula
   - Before/After reveal formula
   Each with a fill-in template and a real example.
```
