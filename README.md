# Home Services & Contractor Marketing Kit Generator

Generate a complete local marketing kit for any home service business — HVAC, plumbing, electrical, roofing, landscaping, pool, pest control, cleaning, or handyman — in under 20 minutes.

## What You Get

4 specialized prompts that cover the entire local marketing lifecycle:

1. **Seasonal Campaign Generator** — Urgency-driven promotions with email sequence, paid ads (Google + Facebook), Nextdoor post, and door hanger copy
2. **Service Page Copy Generator** — SEO-optimized web page: headline, meta, benefits, FAQ, how-it-works, trust signals, CTA
3. **Referral & Review System** — 3-touch review request sequence, QR card, referral program, 6 Google review response templates
4. **30-Day Social Calendar** — 22 posts across Facebook, Instagram, and Nextdoor with evergreen templates and a quick-post cheat sheet

Home service businesses lose customers to competitors with better Google presence and more reviews — not better service. This kit fixes both.

- **Seasonal campaigns** generate 30–60% more bookings vs. "always-on" ads
- **Review systems** with 3-touch follow-up generate 4–6× more reviews than a single ask
- **Nextdoor** converts at 3–5× the rate of Facebook for local home services
- **Service pages** structured with local keywords rank faster in Google Maps and organic search
