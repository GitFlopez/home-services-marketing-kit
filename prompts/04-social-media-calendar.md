## Tips for Best Results

- **Nextdoor is gold for home services.** It converts at 3–5× the rate of Facebook for local service calls. Every week should have at least 1 Nextdoor post. Sound like a neighbor, not an ad.
- **Facebook videos/Reels get 5–10× the organic reach** of static images on Facebook pages. Even a 15-second tip video filmed vertically with the technician's phone outperforms a polished photo.
- **Hashtags don't matter on Facebook.** Use them only on Instagram (10-15 per post: mix local + service + general).
- **Post during peak scroll times.** For home service audiences: Facebook: 6-9AM and 7-9PM. Instagram: 11AM-1PM and 7-9PM. Nextdoor: 7-9AM and 7-9PM (people checking before/after work).

## 30-Day Calendar Execution Tips

1. **Batch-create content on Sundays.** Take 1 hour to write, schedule, and set up next week's posts using Buffer, Hootsuite, or Meta Business Suite (free for Facebook/Instagram).
2. **Every Monday, check last week's reach.** Top-performing post format = make more of that. Bottom performer = try a different format next time.
3. **Nextdoor requires manual posting.** No scheduling tool works for Nextdoor — plan to post manually 1-2× per week.
4. **The technician is the content creator.** Give techs a simple 3-photo habit: before the job (problem visible), during (tech working), after (completed). This generates 2-3 posts per job automatically.
